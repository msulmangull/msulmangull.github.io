Project Description

• KeepForm is an app designed and developed for fitness training guidance.
• The app provides fitness enthusiasts with access to a variety of training guides, instructions, and resources.
• Users can find fitness plans and guidance that suit their preferences and goals.
• The app supports various formats for sharing instructions, including images, PDFs, and text.
• Users can subscribe to the app for a small fee to gain access to a wide range of fitness training content.
• KeepForm empowers users to follow structured fitness plans and receive professional guidance.
• The app eliminates the need for users to search for fitness resources elsewhere.
• It simplifies the process of finding and accessing fitness training materials in one centralized platform.
• KeepForm aims to support individuals who want to improve their fitness but struggle to find reliable and comprehensive guidance.
• By offering a subscription-based model, the app ensures ongoing access to updated and curated fitness content.

Outcomes
KeepForm is an essential tool for anyone looking to take their fitness to the next level. With its extensive library of training guides, intuitive interface, and powerful features, KeepForm has everything you need to achieve your fitness goals and transform your body.
