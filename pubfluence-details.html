Project Description

• Pubfluence is an app designed for restaurants and their staff members.
• It connects restaurants with potential staff members to fill in shifts as needed.
• Staff members can showcase their skills and build a following on the Pubfluence website.
• The app offers flexible working opportunities for staff members to find additional shifts.
• It provides efficient staffing solutions for restaurants, addressing staffing gaps quickly.
• Staff members can network with fellow professionals in the restaurant industry.
• The app enhances customer service by allowing staff members to engage with followers.
• Restaurants gain increased visibility and attract talented staff members through Pubfluence.
• The platform fosters knowledge sharing, industry discussions, and community building.
• Pubfluence aims to revolutionize the restaurant industry through technology and networking.

Outcomes
Pubfluence aims to revolutionize the restaurant industry by providing efficient staffing solutions, empowering staff members to build their personal brands, and fostering connections among industry professionals. By leveraging technology and networking capabilities, Pubfluence strives to enhance operational efficiency, professional growth, and customer satisfaction within the restaurant ecosystem.
