Project Description

• USA Errand Service is an app developed for convenient pickup and delivery services.
• The app caters to users who need to order pickups and drop-offs from any location using their smartphones. It connects users with nearby drivers who can fulfill their transportation needs.
• When a user places a pickup request, the app assigns the job to the nearest available driver within a certain time frame. • If the assigned driver does not respond within the given time, the job is automatically assigned to the next closest driver.
• USA Errand Service offers flexibility by allowing drivers to transfer deliveries to other drivers in the middle of the delivery process.
• Users can track their orders in real-time, providing transparency and visibility throughout the delivery journey. The app captures proof of delivery through images or digital signatures, ensuring accountability.
• Fare calculations in USA Errand Service are based on distance and time, providing a fair and transparent pricing system.
• The app enhances convenience and efficiency for both users and drivers in accessing and providing delivery services.

Outcomes
Erand revolutionizes the pickup and drop-off service industry by leveraging technology to provide a seamless and efficient experience for both users and drivers, leading to improved service, optimized operations, and enhanced customer satisfaction.
