Project Description

• Developed a cutting-edge app called Popyan.
• Utilized NFC technology to revolutionize digital business card management.
• Created a user-friendly interface for the app.
• Enabled users to easily create and customize their digital business cards.
• Integrated NFC chip functionality into physical cards and compatible devices.
• Enabled sharing of digital business cards through NFC scanning.
• Allowed users to add social links, contact details, and other information to their profiles.
• Stored all information in a convenient digital location.
• Facilitated seamless sharing of digital business cards with others.
• Eliminated the need for physical business cards and manual contact information exchange.
• Offered customization options for creating professional-looking digital business cards.
• Provided a platform for networking and connecting with others.
• Enabled instant sharing of contact details and social media profiles through NFC scanning.
• Supported networking efforts by expanding users’ professional networks.
• Helped users build valuable connections in their industry.
• Implemented customizable privacy settings for users to control information sharing.
• Offered advanced analytics features for tracking the number of scans received by digital business cards.
• Provided insights into users’ networking efforts.

Outcomes
An innovative app that is transforming the way professionals manage their digital business cards and connect with others. With its powerful features and intuitive interface, it’s an essential tool for anyone looking to streamline their networking efforts and build their professional brand.
