Project Description

• FoodTruck Roundup is a marketplace for buying and selling food from trucks.
• It is a platform where food truck owners can set up their profiles, locations, and working hours.
Customers can locate and browse nearby food trucks on a map.
• The app allows customers to search for specific food items and join food truck events.
• Customers can place orders for pickup, ensuring their food is ready upon arrival.
• The platform provides food truck owners with tools to manage and promote their events.
• It offers a convenient way for customers to explore and enjoy diverse food truck options.
• FoodTruck Roundup supports a seamless transaction process with integrated payment options.

Outcomes
FoodTruck Roundup aims to enhance the food truck experience for both food truck owners and customers by providing a user-friendly platform for discovery, engagement, and seamless food ordering. This project supports the growth of the food truck industry and encourages community engagement around culinary experiences.

Tech Stack Used
Android App Development with Java
Google Maps and Places API
Firebase
