Project Description

• Built RideAlong, a dynamic and user-centric community-based app.
• Revolutionized the way people travel by providing a platform for ride-sharing.
• Catered to individuals seeking cost-effective and convenient transportation options.
• Offered drivers an opportunity to monetize their journeys.
• Connected passengers in need of rides with drivers heading in the same direction.
• Created a mutually beneficial system for both passengers and drivers.
• Enabled passengers to secure rides through the app.
• Helped drivers maximize their resources and reduce travel costs.
• Applied Android/Kotlin, Kotlin, Android SDK, and Android skills.

Outcomes
RideAlong revolutionizes the way people travel by providing a community-driven platform for ride-sharing. By offering cost-effective travel options, reducing environmental impact, and fostering social connections, the app aims to enhance the overall travel experience and promote a sustainable and efficient transportation system.

Tech Stack Used
Android App Development with Kotlin
Google Maps and Places API
Firebase
