Project description

• Team Me is a sports event management app designed to streamline the process of hosting, managing, and joining sports events.
• Users can create and host their own events, providing details such as event type, date, location, and any specific requirements.
• The app allows event organizers to manage event members, track attendance, and communicate with participants through a group chat feature.
• Users can browse and join events posted by others, enabling them to connect with like-minded sports enthusiasts and expand their network.
• The app incorporates a friend request system similar to Facebook, allowing users to connect with each other and build connections within the sports community.
• Team Me maintains a history of past events, providing users with a comprehensive overview of their sports-related activities and achievements.
• Users can send requests to join specific events, providing organizers with a seamless process to manage participant registration and approval.
• The app aims to enhance the overall sports event experience by facilitating communication, networking, and efficient event management.

Outcomes
The “Team Me” sports event management app aims to revolutionize the way sports events are organized and participated in. By providing a user-friendly platform for hosting, managing, and joining sports events, the app strives to enhance the overall sports event experience. The outcomes of this project include increased convenience and efficiency in event organization, seamless communication among event members through the group chat feature, expanded networks and connections within the sports community, and a comprehensive record of past events and achievements. With its focus on streamlining event management processes and fostering community engagement, “Team Me” has the potential to bring together sports enthusiasts, create memorable experiences, and promote a thriving sports ecosystem.
