Project Description

• Developed SERO, an event management app specifically designed for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
• Catered to three types of users: Admins, Organizers, and normal users.
• Gave Admins the authority to manage Organizers within the app.
• Entrusted Organizers with creating and managing events through the app.
• Enabled normal users to explore and discover events using various search filters.
• Provided location-based searches on a map and specific date-based searches for users.
• Facilitated ticket purchases through Apple Pay or credit cards.
• Included a companion Watch OS app for Apple Watch in SERO.
• Allowed parents to track their children at large events using the Watch app.
• Implemented a system where children wore a connected Watch sending their coordinates to the app’s database.
• Enabled parents to monitor their child’s location in real time through the app.
• Built the app using SwiftUI and Swift programming languages.

Outcomes
SERO serves as a comprehensive event management app, empowering organizers to seamlessly manage events, providing a convenient ticket-purchasing experience for users, and ensuring the safety of children through the Apple Watch companion app.
