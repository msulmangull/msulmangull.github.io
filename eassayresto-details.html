Project Description

• Developed EassyResto, a project designed to bridge the gap between restaurants and customers.
• Created two apps: one for restaurants and one for users.
• Simplified the process of finding suitable restaurants for users.
• Implemented features such as reviews, menu observation, and a user-friendly interface.
• Enabled users to explore various restaurants and read reviews from other customers.
• Provided menus for users to make informed decisions about their dining choices.
• Allowed users to reserve tables effortlessly through the app.
• Eliminated the need for phone calls or in-person visits for table reservations.
• Developed the Eassy Resto app as a valuable management tool for restaurants.
• Provided functionalities for restaurant owners to efficiently handle reservations and track bookings.
• Helped optimize restaurant operations through the app’s features.
• Created a platform for restaurant owners to showcase their establishments.
• Included menu listings and other relevant information to attract potential customers.
• Enhanced visibility of restaurants through the app’s exposure.

Outcomes
The EassyResto project aims to revolutionize the way users discover and reserve tables at restaurants while providing restaurant owners with effective management tools.

Tech Stack Used
Android App Development with kotlin
Firebase
Payment Processing using Paypal SDK
